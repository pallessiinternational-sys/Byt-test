CodeByt Policies

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

CodeByt is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

1. Information Collection & Usage

CodeByt operates on a zero-data-collection principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data processed through our cryptographic algorithms.

2. Local Processing Architecture

All cryptographic operations are performed locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. This approach eliminates network-based vulnerabilities and ensures your information remains under your control.

3. Session & Storage Management

We store minimal session data locally on your device (browser storage) to maintain:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Command history for terminal operations
  • Encryption key preferences (locally encrypted)
  • Theme and interface settings

This data can be cleared at any time through your browser settings and is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services & Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The platform operates independently without external dependencies. No cookies are used for tracking purposes.

5. Contact Information Handling

If you choose to contact us through our contact form, we only collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) for the sole purpose of responding to your inquiry. This information is kept confidential and is not shared with third parties.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Since we don't store your data on our servers, there's no data retention period. All data processing occurs locally and is immediately discarded after your session ends, unless you explicitly choose to save preferences locally.

7. Security Measures

  • End-to-end local encryption processing
  • No server-side data storage
  • Regular security audits of our codebase
  • Transparent open-source verification available
Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we might hold. Since we don't store personal data, your privacy is inherently protected by our architecture.

Documentation & Usage Guidelines

Comprehensive Platform Guide

This documentation provides complete information about using CodeByt Cryptography Platform effectively and securely.

1. Available Algorithms

CodeByt provides 15 verified cryptographic algorithms:

  • Neural Network Encryption - AI-based adaptive encryption
  • Quantum Vortex Algorithm - Quantum mechanics-inspired encryption
  • Fractal Recursion Cipher - Multi-scale fractal pattern encryption
  • Chaos Theory Encryption - Deterministic chaotic encryption
  • Tesseract 4D Transformation - Multi-dimensional encryption
  • Quantum Entanglement - Simulated quantum encryption
  • Fibonacci Sequence Encryption - Mathematical sequence-based encryption
  • Matrix Transformation - Linear algebra-based encryption
  • XOR Double Encryption - Dual-layer XOR security
  • ROT13 & ROT47 Ciphers - Classic rotation ciphers
  • Base64 & Base64URL - Binary-to-text encoding
  • Reverse & Atbash Ciphers - Classical substitution ciphers

2. Terminal Commands Reference

Available terminal commands (type 'help' for full list):

  • help - Display all available commands
  • encode [algorithm] [text] - Encode text with specified algorithm
  • decode [algorithm] [text] - Decode text with specified algorithm
  • algorithms - List all available algorithms
  • test [algorithm] - Test an algorithm with sample text
  • benchmark - Run performance benchmark
  • session - Display session information
  • clear - Clear terminal screen
  • history - Show command history
  • strength [text] - Analyze encryption strength
  • keys - Show current encryption keys
  • genkey [type] - Generate random key
  • setkey [type] [key] - Set encryption key
  • theme [dark/light] - Change terminal theme
  • exit - Exit terminal and return to homepage

3. Performance Specifications

  • Average Response Time: <1 millisecond per operation
  • Throughput Capacity: 50,000+ operations/second
  • Algorithm Count: 15 verified algorithms
  • Platform Availability: 24/7 with no downtime
  • Browser Compatibility: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Mobile Support: Fully responsive design for mobile devices

4. Quick Start Guide

  1. Click "Launch Terminal" to access the cryptographic terminal
  2. Type 'help' to see available commands
  3. Use 'encode neural HelloWorld' to test neural encryption
  4. Use 'algorithms' to see all available encryption methods
  5. Use 'benchmark' to test platform performance
